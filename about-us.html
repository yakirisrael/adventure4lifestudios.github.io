An independent game development studio that was founded by Yakir Israel in 2015.
At these days, the studio is working hard on Plot of the Druid, a fantasy point and click adventure
 and the studio's first commercial game.  

Team

Yakir Israel

CEO, Director, Writer, Designer, Programmer
An enthusiastic retro lover, has a sweat spot for all kind of adventure games.

By holding B.Sc degree in computers engineering, Yakir had the opportunity to work at hi-tech by day, and joining the games industry by night.

He also contributed to a non-profit fans project, and remastered Indiana Jones and the fate of Atlantis, one of his favorite adventure games.

Many projects led by Yakir, but this was the funniest. So he decided to leave the hi-tech world and fullfil his dream -  making video games!

Aaron Loherlein
